Our Story

Built by a trader who was tired of losing the signal.

Thirteen years of taking before/after screenshots, buried in folders and drives. The patterns were thereI just couldn't reliably find them. EdgeBook exists so every trader can surface their edge without digging through a messy bucket of files.

Why I built this

After more than a decade trading, my screenshots kept vanishing into unlabeled folders and random Google Drive buckets. When I wanted to revisit a setup, I spent more time hunting than learning. I built EdgeBook to make chart journaling fast, structured, and actually reviewable for all of us.

Inspired by journal-first traders

A big push came from hearing trader Christian Coolamage talk about building a journal for himself. It reinforced what every pro says: the discipline of capturing and reviewing trades is the edge. This app is my answer to making that discipline effortless.
